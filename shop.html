Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Premium

Can I cancel my premium subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect immediately and you will continue to enjoy premium features for the remainder of your paid period.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade between plans at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the price difference; when downgrading, the new plan rate will be applied at your next billing cycle.

Can I use premium features on all my devices?

Yes, your premium subscription is tied to your account. You can enjoy premium features on all your devices (computer, tablet, phone) where you use Chatlify.

What payment methods are available?

We support various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and mobile payments. Cryptocurrency payment is also available in certain regions.
